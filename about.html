  • 01

    Welcome to Eyedeal Vision, where we bring professional contact lens distribution to Kerala, India. Our journey began in 2002 with a passion for supporting optical businesses and ensuring access to high-quality contact lenses. From our carefully selected brand partnerships to our reliable delivery network, every aspect of Eyedeal Vision is designed to support the success of optical stores across Kerala.

  • 02

    Our comprehensive services include wholesale contact lens distribution, retailer supply management, brand partnerships, and bulk order support. We work with leading global and national contact lens manufacturers to provide authentic, high-quality products with proper certifications. Each product is carefully selected to meet the diverse needs of optical professionals and their customers across Kerala.

  • 03

    At Eyedeal Vision, we believe in creating more than just business relationships – we create lasting partnerships. Our commitment to excellence, combined with over 20 years of experience in the optical field, makes us a trusted partner for optical stores across Kerala. Whether you're a small optical shop or a large retail chain, we're committed to providing reliable supply chain solutions and exceptional customer service.
